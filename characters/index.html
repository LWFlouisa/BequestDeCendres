Family Lineage

Your main character is descended from a procedurally generated family tree node each time you play, so it makes hard to predict who exactly you'll play as.

But I can give you a rough idea of character classes:

Classes

The primary class that you'll run into is the hunter class, and everythingin the game will be structured around this rogue hunter. You might sometimes run into a princess, but unlike in some games you job isn't necessarily to rescue her or prevent from being executed.

Sometimes you'll also be descended from a royal member of that lineage, in which case you might play as the princess whom risks execution. I haven't entirely determined at this point whether allow one to rescue oneself is delaying in inevitable, as Roguelikes rely more on procedural generation than most games.

Structure of sequels

Because of this informal gameplay structure, the game will be periodically updated rather than having a normal sequel structure. Although eventually the idea is to have each update have a new MC whose entire backstore is built around death and rememberance in some way related to other protagonists.
