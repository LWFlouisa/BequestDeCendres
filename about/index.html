ABOUT

In an age when science mixes with mysticism, there are regions that concentrate an unusual amount of strange apparitions. In a Cerederinhos, or hospitals that become Yokanozen after strange events, we are introduced to strange test subjects with angel-like and bear-like abilities, and must travel docks and buoys to investigate the institution of drowning.
