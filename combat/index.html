Stealth Mechanics

This game functions like a Stealth text adventure, and eventually will flesh out RPG elements over time.

Commands

Ereil - Allows the player to lure the monster or enemy in your direction.

Eblouir - Allows the player to stun the enemy, which will eventually evolve into elemental types.

Plege - Allows the player to trick the monster or enemy into a trap.

Enemy Behaviours

I'm currently working in the ability for the enemy to do quips based on encountering the player before.

I am also decided between full procedural generation for enemy names or pre rendered generated names.
